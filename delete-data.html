Data & Account Deletion — The Ledger Report

App: The Ledger Report (package com.anonymous.stocktrackergame)

There is no account to delete

The Ledger Report does not have accounts. There is no sign-up, no login, and no profile stored on any server. Everything about your playthrough lives on your own device.

Delete all of your data — instantly, yourself

  1. Open The Ledger Report on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (the gear icon on the title screen).
  3. Tap Erase all data and confirm.

This permanently deletes every save, every completed-character record, and every preference — there is no copy anywhere else. Uninstalling the app has the same effect.

Anonymous analytics

The app records anonymous usage statistics (see our Privacy Policy). These events are not linked to your identity, so they cannot be looked up or deleted per person. To unlink past events from your device, reset your Android advertising ID (Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Reset advertising ID").

Anything else

For purchase refunds, use Google Play's standard refund process (purchases are handled entirely by Google Play). For any other deletion request or privacy question, contact us through the developer contact options on the app's Google Play listing — we respond within 30 days.
