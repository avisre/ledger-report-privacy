Data & Account Deletion

App: The Ledger Report (package com.anonymous.stocktrackergame)

There is no account to delete

The game has no sign-up, no login, and no profile stored on any server. Everything lives on your device, which means you can delete all of your data instantly, yourself — no request, no waiting period.

Delete everything in three steps

  1. Open The Ledger Report on your device.
  2. Go to Settings (the gear icon on the title screen).
  3. Tap Erase all data and confirm.

This permanently deletes every save, every completed-character record, and every preference — there is no copy anywhere else. Uninstalling the app has the same effect.

What about analytics?

The app records anonymous gameplay statistics via Google Firebase Analytics (see the Privacy Policy for exactly what is collected). Because these events are anonymous, we cannot identify which ones belong to you. To unlink past analytics events from your device, reset your Android advertising ID:

Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset advertising ID

Anything else?

If you have a deletion request the steps above don't cover, contact us — we respond to deletion requests within 30 days, usually much sooner.
