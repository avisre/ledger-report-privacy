Privacy Policy

App: The Ledger Report (package com.anonymous.stocktrackergame)
Effective date: June 10, 2026

The short version

The Ledger Report is a single-player story game. It has no account, no login, no ads, and no social features. Your game progress is stored only on your device. The app collects anonymous gameplay statistics to help us improve the story — never your name, email, contacts, messages, photos, or location.

What we collect

From version 1.0.0 (38) onward, the app uses Google Firebase Analytics to record anonymous usage events, limited to:

  • App interactions — e.g. which protagonist was started, which act a player reached, whether the unlock screen was viewed, and whether a purchase was completed or restored.
  • Device or other identifiers — a resettable device identifier (including the Android advertising ID) and basic device information (model, OS version, country, language), used by Firebase to count users and sessions.

These statistics are not linked to your identity. We do not collect, and the app contains no means to collect, your name, email address, phone number, contacts, precise location, photos, files, or the content of anything you type.

On Apple iOS (App Store), analytics are disabled — the iOS version of the app collects no analytics or usage data at all.

What we never do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share data with third parties for advertising or marketing.
  • We do not show ads, and the game contains no third-party ad SDKs.
  • We do not require or create user accounts.

Purchases

The optional one-time purchase ("Unlock the full story") is processed entirely by the platform's app store — Google Play Billing on Android and the Apple App Store on iOS. We never see your payment details. Your entitlement is restored through the same store account via "Restore purchases" on a new device.

Game saves & your right to be forgotten

All story progress lives in local storage on your device. You can permanently erase every trace of it at any time — see Delete My Data for the step-by-step instructions. Uninstalling the app also removes all local data.

Data processing & retention

Anonymous analytics are processed by Google LLC on our behalf (see Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase Privacy & Security). Analytics data is retained per Firebase's standard retention settings and is used solely to understand aggregate gameplay (e.g. "how many players finish Act 1") so we can improve the game.

Children

The Ledger Report is a historical narrative game intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children; the app collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If we change what the app collects, we will update this page and the app's Google Play Data safety section before the change takes effect.
